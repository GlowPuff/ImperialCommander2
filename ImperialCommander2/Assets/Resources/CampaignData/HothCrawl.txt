Grim tidings arrive for the Rebellion; their base upon the surface of Hoth has been discovered. Led by the fearsome DARTH VADER, the Empire launches a massive assault upon Rebel forces on the ice planet, ready to crush them once and for all.

Barely able to withstand the onslaught, the Rebellion scrambles to evacuate the frigid world and escape the clutches of the Empire once again. As transports and fighters frantically prepare their departure, a few brave Rebel soldiers risk their lives against hopeless odds to buy them precious seconds...
