<b>Information:</b>
Refer to the mission rules for instructions for the <b>Imperial AT-ATs</b>.

<b>Special Setup:</b>
Add all figures with "Massive" to the ignored groups.

<b>Repositioning Target:</b>
Block access to the terminals.

<b>Rebel Victory:</b>
No <b>Imperial AT-AT</b> left on the Battleground.

<b>Imperial Victory:</b>
A <b>Rebel AT-AT</b> is defeated, end of Round 5 before <color="red">Commandeered</color> is resolved, or all heroes are wounded.

<b>At the end Round 1:</b> <color="red">Intruder Alert!</color>

<b>When the first door opens:</b> <color="red">Foresection Entered</color>

<b>When the second door opens:</b> <color="red">Cockpit Breached</color>

<b>When a hero takes control of the walker:</b> <color="red">Commandeered</color>
