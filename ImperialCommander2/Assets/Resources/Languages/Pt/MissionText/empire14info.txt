<b>Special Setup:</b>
Do not draw cards to determine the Shuttle Guard. Use random deployments instead. (See event information below.)

<b>Special Rules:</b>
Imperial figures do not use the elevator.

<b>Repositioning Target:</b>
Be on or adjacent to <b>Senator Feralo</b> and block access to the shuttle.

<b>Rebel Victory:</b>
The shuttle launches.

<b>Imperial Victory:</b>
At the end of Round 6, <b>Senator Feralo</b> is arrested, or all heroes are wounded.

<b>First time a Rebel figure is on the Roof:</b> <color="red">Backup has Arrived</color>. From now on, distribute deployments evenly between the Roof and the Penthouse.

<b>First time a Rebel figure enters the Shuttle:</b> <color="red">Secure the Shuttle</color> (see mission rules)
