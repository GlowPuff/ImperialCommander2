<b>Special Setup:</b>
Do not draw cards to determine the Cantina Patrol or the Junk Shop Patrol. Use random deployments instead. (See event information below.)

<b>Repositioning Target:</b>
Block access to doors, informants, and terminals.

<b>Rebel Victory:</b>
All informants and terminals are claimed.

<b>Imperial Victory:</b>
At the end of Round 7, or all heroes are wounded and <color="red">R2-D2</color> is defeated.

<b>First time a door to the Cantina opens:</b> <color="red">Cantina Patrol</color>. Perform a random deployment with a maximum threat cost of twice the threat level.

<b>First time a door to the Junk Shop opens:</b> <color="red">Junk Shop</color>. Perform a random deployment with a maximum threat cost of twice the threat level.

<b>2 informants and/or terminals have been claimed:</b> <color="red">Reinforcements</color>
