<b>Special Setup:</b>
Deploy <color="red">IG-88</color> as a Initial Group, but set aside his figure.

<b>Special Rules:</b>
When <color="red">IG-88</color> activates, he and all of his copies activate at the same time, starting with the real <color="red">IG-88</color>, then with the copy closest to the Rebels, then with the second copy. The copies use <color="red">IG-88's</color> instructions, but do not have the "Assault" ability.

<b>Repositioning Target:</b>
Block access to the doors.

<b>Rebel Victory:</b>
<color="red">IG-88</color> and both copies of <b>IG-88</b> are defeated.

<b>Imperial Victory:</b>
At the end of Round 6, or all heroes are wounded.

<b>First time a door opens:</b> <color="red">Droid Uprising</color>

<b>At the end of the round in which <color="red">Droid Uprising</color> resolves:</b> <color="red">Mass Produced</color>. Deploy <color="red">IG-88's</color> set-aside figure. This is the real <color="red">IG-88</color>.
