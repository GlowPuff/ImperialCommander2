<b>Mission instructions:</b>
When less than 6 strain tokens are on <color="red">Emperor Palpatine's</color> deployment card, <color="red">Emperor Palpatine</color> uses the following instructions:

■ EMPEROR: The other Imperial figure with the highest figure cost that can attack attacks <<the closest healthy Rebel>>.
<font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">A</font> If this figure is at least 5 spaces away from the shard, it moves 3 toward the shard.
<font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">A</font> FORCE LIGHTNING: One Rebel within 4 spaces suffers 2 <font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">H</font> and becomes Weakened. If there is another Rebel within 2 spaces of the target, repeat this instruction targeting that Rebel. Limit once per Rebel.
<font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">Q</font> Move 2 to attack <<the closest healthy Rebel>>.
<font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">A</font> Move 3 to reposition 3.

When 6 or more strain tokens are on <color="red">Emperor Palpatine's</color> deployment card, <color="red">Emperor Palpatine</color> uses the following instructions:

■ If TEMPT is not the bonus effect, use TEMPT: At the start of this activation, the hero with the least <font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">J</font> dice tests <font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">J</font>. If he or she fails, that hero attacks another Rebel of his or her choice. After this attack, the attacker gain 2 <font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">h</font>.
■ If DARK RAGE is not the bonus effect, use DARK RAGE: At the start of this activation, the other Imperial figure with the highest cost gains 1 <font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">h</font>. If that figure has the <font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">P</font> attack type, it gains 2 <font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">h</font> instead.
<font="ImperialAssaultSymbols SDF">AA</font> Remove 1 strain token from your deployment card.
